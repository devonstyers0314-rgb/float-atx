About Float ATX

Born on Lake Travis. Built for Austin.

Our Story

Float ATX started with one simple idea: make a day on Lake Travis as easy and accessible as possible. No boat ownership headaches, no trailer, no storage fees. Just show up, get on the water, and have a great day.

We specialize exclusively in the Sea-Doo Switch Cruise because we genuinely believe it's the best rental experience on the lake. It's stable, fun, easy to operate, and unlike anything else out there.

About the Sea-Doo Switch Cruise

  • Seats up to 8 passengers comfortably
  • 130 HP Rotax engine
  • Pontoon-style layout with modular seating
  • Top speed around 40 mph
  • Extremely stable — great for all ages and skill levels
  • Built-in swim platform and boarding ladder
  • Bluetooth audio system

Safety Protocols

  1. All renters must be 18 or older with a valid government-issued ID.
  2. A full safety briefing is conducted before every rental, no exceptions.
  3. US Coast Guard-approved life jackets are provided for every passenger.
  4. No alcohol consumption while operating the vessel — Texas law and our policy.
  5. Renters are responsible for following all Texas Parks & Wildlife boating regulations.
  6. A safety kill switch must be worn by the operator at all times while underway.
  7. Weather conditions are monitored — unsafe conditions result in rescheduling.

How Booking Works

  1. Contact us by email to check your preferred date and time.
  2. We'll confirm availability and send you a reservation form.
  3. A deposit is required to hold your reservation.
  4. Show up on the day with your ID and remaining balance.
  5. Complete the safety briefing and sign the liability waiver.
  6. Get on the water and enjoy your day.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a boating license?

A Texas boating license is required by the primary renter to rent from us. We provide a full safety briefing before every trip.

How many people can ride?

The Sea-Doo Switch Cruise seats up to 8 passengers comfortably.

What's included in the rental?

Life jackets for all passengers, a safety briefing, and a full tank of gas are included.

Can we bring food and drinks?

Yes! Non-alcoholic beverages and food are welcome. No glass containers on the boat, please.

What happens if the weather is bad?

We monitor conditions closely. If it's unsafe to go out, we'll reschedule at no penalty to you.

Is there a security deposit?

Yes, a refundable security deposit is collected at the time of rental and returned upon safe return of the vessel.

Where exactly do we launch from?

We launch from Lake Travis in Austin, TX. Exact location details are shared upon booking confirmation.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 48+ hours in advance receive a full deposit refund. Late cancellations may forfeit the deposit.
